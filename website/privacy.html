Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

TailorFit AI is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We believe your professional data and career history should remain entirely under your control.

1. Data Storage

The extension stores your resume content, job application history, and API keys locally in your browser's dedicated storage (Chrome Storage API). We do not own, operate, or maintain any external databases that store your personal information.

2. Data Processing

When you use the "Tailor Resume" feature, the extension sends your resume text and the job description to the Google Gemini API. This communication happens directly between your browser and Google's servers using the API key you provide. We do not intercept, view, or store this data.

3. Use of Permissions

  • activeTab: Used exclusively to read the job description on the page you are currently viewing.
  • storage: Used to keep your settings and resume data persistent on your machine.
  • scripting: Used to identify and extract relevant job details (Title, Company, Description) from web pages.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your data is only shared with Google's Gemini AI service as part of the core functionality of the extension.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via the GitHub repository where this project is hosted.
